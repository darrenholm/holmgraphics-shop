Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. Here's how we handle your information.

Last updated: April 15, 2026
🔒 Holm Graphics Inc. does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. Ever.

This Privacy Policy describes how Holm Graphics Inc. ("Holm Graphics," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you visit holmgraphics.ca, place an order, or interact with our services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Name, email address, phone number, and mailing/shipping address when you place an order or request a quote
  • Payment information (processed securely through our payment processor — we do not store full card numbers)
  • Artwork, logos, and design files you submit for production
  • Communications you send us via email, phone, or contact forms

We may also automatically collect basic technical information such as your browser type and pages visited, solely for the purpose of improving our website.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Process and fulfill your orders
  • Send order confirmations, invoices, and status updates
  • Respond to your questions and requests
  • Improve our products and services
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not use your information for automated decision-making or profiling.

3. Accounting & Financial Integrations

Holm Graphics uses QuickBooks Online to manage invoicing and accounting. When you place an order, basic order details (your name, email, items purchased, and order total) may be recorded in our QuickBooks account for bookkeeping purposes. This data is handled in accordance with Intuit's privacy practices. We do not share your financial details with any other third parties.

4. Sharing Your Information

We only share your information with trusted third parties where necessary to operate our business:

  • Payment processors — to securely handle transactions
  • Shipping carriers — to deliver your orders
  • Accounting software (QuickBooks) — for invoicing and bookkeeping
  • Legal authorities — if required by law

All third-party service providers are required to handle your data securely and only for the purpose we specify.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, or as required by law. Order records are typically retained for seven years for tax and accounting purposes. You may request deletion of your data at any time (subject to legal retention obligations) by contacting us.

6. Your Rights (PIPEDA)

As a Canadian business, we comply with the Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA). You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request corrections to inaccurate information
  • Withdraw consent for non-essential uses of your data
  • Request deletion of your information (where legally permissible)

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at darren@holmgraphics.ca.

7. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. Our online shop uses SSL encryption for all transactions. However, no method of internet transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Cookies

Our website may use basic cookies to improve your browsing experience. We do not use tracking cookies for advertising purposes. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated policy on this page with a revised date. Continued use of our services after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
