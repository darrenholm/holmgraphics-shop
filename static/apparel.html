Decoration Methods

We offer multiple decoration techniques to achieve the perfect look for your project

DTF (Direct to Film)

Modern digital printing technique perfect for full-color designs with excellent durability

  • Full-color capability
  • Photographic quality
  • Works on most fabrics
  • No minimum order
  • Durable and long-lasting

Screen Printing

Traditional method ideal for large quantities and solid color designs

  • Best for bulk orders
  • Vibrant solid colors
  • Cost-effective for volume
  • Long-lasting results
  • Professional finish
